Intake Manifold

  • Definition: the part of an engine that supplies the fuel/air mixture to the cylinders.

 

  • High-performance manifold:
  • Large plenum
  • Oversized runners
  • Fewer bends (minor loss)
  • Runner exit match cylinder head (minor loss)
